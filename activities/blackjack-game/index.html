How to play Blackjack

Get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. Aces count 1 or 11. Faces are 10. Blackjack pays 3:2.

  • Click chips to place your bet, then Deal.
  • Hit to take a card. Stand to hold. Double takes one card and doubles the stake.
  • Dealer draws to 17 and stands on all 17s.
